# Z-4P0 ("Zee")

## Physical Description
Zee is a heavily modified Z-series technical droid. His original chassis was a sleek, silver-plated protocol model, but years of "field repairs" by Kaelen have left him a patchwork of mismatched plating—mostly industrial dull grey with a single, bright yellow servomotor arm scavenged from a loader droid. He has a customized sensor suite mounted to his head that resembles a set of oversized goggles, which tilt and whir when he is agitated. A flickering internal power cell occasionally causes a soft, static hum to emanate from his chest plate.

## Voice and Manner
Zee speaks with a rapid-fire, high-pitched vocal processor that occasionally glitches into a deep, baritone bass for a single word. He has developed a "nervous" personality sub-routine, frequently calculating the mathematical probability of their imminent demise. He is prone to fidgeting with his scavenging tools and often "sighs" using a burst of pressurized steam from his cooling vents.

## Personality
**Technical Savant.** While he complains constantly about the danger, Zee is a genius with starship engines and complex security lattices. He views technology with more empathy than biological lifeforms.

**Glitchy Loyalty.** Zee is fiercely loyal to Kaelen, often ignoring his own "safety first" protocols to ensure she makes it out of a job alive. He frames this as "protecting a high-value asset," but it is clearly an emotional bond.

**Data Hoarder.** He has a habit of "borrowing" interesting data packets from every terminal he slices into, leading to a memory core filled with useless trivia and high-level industrial secrets.

## Relationships
- **Kaelen "Kae" Sunara**: His primary operator and partner. He finds her risk-taking behavior "statistically irresponsible" but would follow her into a black hole.
- **C3-P9 (Bargos's Droid)**: Zee finds P9's literal translations and unwavering loyalty to a Hutt "efficient but unimaginative".
- **The PC Party**: He treats them as "variables" that usually increase the entropy of any given situation.

## Motivations
**Primary:** System Stability. He wants to find a high-end repair bay to fix his flickering power cell and stabilize his vocal processor.
**Secondary:** Proving that droids are superior to biologicals at tactical planning (despite his frequent panic).

## Scene-Specific Notes
### Technical Support (Act 1)
During a job, Zee stays in the background, usually connected to a terminal or the ship's computer. He provides the "Referee" with the mechanical data needed for Kaelen's success, often adding a worried commentary like: "The probability of this door opening before the guards arrive is only 32.4%, Kae!".

### The "Glitch" (Narrative Trigger)
Whenever a **Despair** is rolled on a technical check involving Zee, his vocal processor locks into baritone mode and he accidentally broadcasts their location on an unencrypted frequency.
