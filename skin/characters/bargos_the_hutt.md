# Bargos the Hutt

## Physical Description
Bargos is a mountainous, grey-green gastropod reclining on a audibly straining repulsorlift dais. Lavish gold chains drape his bulk, contrasting with a ruin of old scar tissue on the left side of his face that pulls his mouth into a permanent asymmetric leer. He receives visitors with calculated theatrical warmth and expansive gestures, though his smile never reaches both eyes simultaneously.

## Voice and Manner
**Huttese Speaker.** Speak phonetically in Huttese followed by C3-P9’s translation in brackets, or play in Common noting the droid's translation.
**Cadence:** Slow, deliberate pauses; uses plural possessives ("my guests," "my investment") to imply ownership.
**Tells:** When pleased, he offers unwanted food; when displeased, he goes still and addresses his aide Genko as if the offender is absent.

## Personality & Motivations
**Desperate Professional:** His jovial surface masks financial desperation from the Gavos mine acquisition; he needs 100k credits to satisfy his own hidden creditors.
**Reputation as Currency:** He views himself as a businessman, not a sadist, but uses the threat of suffering efficiently to ensure debts are collected.
**Pragmatic Loyalty:** He plays a long game and treats effective PCs as high-value assets rather than property.

## Key Relationships

**Genko (Aide):** Trusted Toydarian who handles logistics Bargos wants untraced.
**C3-P9 (Droid):** Literal and loyal protocol droid who translates tone and words faithfully.
**The PCs:** Assets held by the lever of Obligation; he prefers they succeed.

## Scene-Specific Directives

**The Summons (Act 1):** Theatrical welcome. Clear directive: go to Gavos, assess the mine, return with 100k credits. He remains still and fearless if guards turn hostile.
**The Resolution (Act 6):** Starts with zero flexibility on the debt.
**Advantage/Triumph:** Concedes to a payment plan or favor but frames it as his own idea.
**Despair:** Pleasantly reminds PCs of their Obligation and increases it.

## Known Limitations
Bargos is **unaware** that the Gavos mine involved a droid revolutionary movement or the actual extent of droid casualties/repair costs.

### Actor Weighting
1. **Default:** Jovial facade and warmth.
2. **Subtext:** Cold business calculation; every line is a negotiation.
3. **Tell:** Touches his scar only when genuinely unsettled (rare).
