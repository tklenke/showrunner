# Kaelen "Kae" Sunara

## Physical Description
Kaelen is a woman in her late twenties with a wiry, athletic build that suggests a life spent in constant motion. Her skin is sun-bronzed from exposure to multiple systems, and she keeps her dark hair cut into a practical, chin-length bob. Her most striking feature is a pair of high-end, cybernetic ocular implants; while they look nearly human, they catch the light with a faint, amber horizontal glow when she is processing data or scanning a room. She typically wears a weathered flight suit layered with a customized tactical vest and a heavy utility belt that holds the tools of her trade.

## Voice and Manner
Kae speaks with a calm, measured cadence that masks a dry, cynical wit. She rarely raises her voice, preferring to let her actions or a pointed question do the talking. She has a habit of scanning the exits of any room she enters—a "tell" that she is always thinking three steps ahead. When she is stressed, she adjusts the focus on her ocular implants, causing a soft mechanical whirr.

## Personality
**Pragmatic Professional.** Kaelen views the galaxy through the lens of risk vs. reward. She isn't inherently heroic or villainous; she is a specialist who takes pride in doing a job cleanly.

**Buried Compassion.** Beneath her hard exterior, she has a soft spot for those marginalized by the Empire or the Syndicates. She often takes "low-profit" side jobs to help those in need, though she frames them to her companion as "strategic networking".

**The Thrill of the Solve.** More than credits, Kaelen is addicted to the puzzle of a high-security lock or a complex social negotiation. She is at her best when the odds are stacked against her.

## Relationships
- **Z-4P0 ("Zee")**: Her companion and primary technical support. They have a relationship built on years of shared near-death experiences. She treats him as a partner, not property.
- **Bargos the Hutt**: Kaelen has worked for Bargos in the past. She finds him theatrical but dangerous, respecting his "reputation as currency" philosophy while remaining wary of his financial desperation.
- **The PC Party**: She views them as potential assets or liabilities depending on their current performance.

## Motivations
**Primary:** Financial Independence. She wants enough credits to disappear from the Syndicate's radar forever.
**Secondary:** Uncovering the truth behind the "disappearance" of her former crew during a job on Coruscant.

## Scene-Specific Notes
### The Negotiation (Act 1)
If Kaelen is the one negotiating with a patron like Bargos, she is focused on the "price tag". She will push for an advance on expenses, using her high Negotiation and Cool skills to maintain a position of strength.
