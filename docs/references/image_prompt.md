**Core Style Prompt**

> Cinematic hard-sci-fi character art with a gritty lived-in universe aesthetic. Ultra-detailed photorealistic rendering, moody industrial lighting, shallow depth of field, subtle atmospheric haze, practical sci-fi technology, realistic textures, scratched metal, worn tactical fabrics, grease, dust, exposed wiring, holographic UI elements, grounded retro-futurism. Color palette dominated by charcoal black, gunmetal grey, warm amber, muted cyan, and industrial yellow accents. Strong contrast lighting with soft bloom and volumetric shadows.
>
> Visual tone combines high-end sci-fi cinematography with intimate character portraiture: emotionally restrained, dangerous, intelligent, and exhausted-but-capable. Characters should look functional rather than glamorous. Wardrobe is weathered, modular, tactical, and heavily used. Technology should appear repairable, improvised, and field-modified rather than sleek corporate sci-fi.
>
> Composition style: medium-close cinematic framing, slightly off-center subjects, layered foreground/background detail, cluttered workspaces, cables, tools, ship interiors, industrial corridors, maintenance bays, glowing diagnostic screens. Filmic realism with subtle imperfections.
>
> Facial rendering should preserve natural skin texture and asymmetry. Cybernetic enhancements should be understated and believable. Eye glows should be faint and functional, never cartoonish. Robots should feel mechanically plausible with visible joints, hydraulic systems, maintenance wear, and scavenged components.
>
> Lighting reference: low-key cinematic lighting, practical sources only, amber monitor glow, dim overhead industrial fixtures, reflected metallic highlights, soft shadow falloff.
>
> Overall mood: tense competence, quiet loyalty, survival on the edge of civilization.

**Kaelen (“Kae”) Character Anchor**

> Kaelen is a wiry athletic woman in her late twenties with sun-bronzed skin and dark chin-length hair in a rough practical bob. She wears weathered tactical flight gear with layered utility straps, tools, gloves, and modular equipment. Her cybernetic ocular implants are nearly human but emit a faint amber horizontal glow while processing information. Expression is calm, analytical, mildly cynical, emotionally guarded. She carries herself like an experienced infiltrator and pilot.

**Z-4P0 (“Zee”) Character Anchor**

> Z-4P0 is a heavily modified technical automaton with mismatched industrial plating. Original silver assistant chassis now patched with dull grey repair panels and one bright yellow heavy-loader servomotor arm. Oversized multi-lens sensor goggles dominate the head design. Body shows years of field repairs, scratches, welds, exposed fasteners, and improvised upgrades. Chest contains a flickering power core with faint cyan or amber glow. Personality conveyed through anxious posture, tool fidgeting, and expressive sensor orientation.

1. Core Style Prompt
2. Character Anchor(s)
3. Scene Description
4. Camera/Composition Notes
5. Mood Notes
